About the Event
RIDE. INSPIRE. CHANGE.
A celebration of women-led change, one pedal at a time.

The Women Led Cyclothon 2026 is back with its 3rd edition — bigger, bolder, and more impactful than ever. Organised in the heart of Jaipur, this event brings together cyclists of all ages and abilities to pedal for a cause.

Whether you're a seasoned rider or picking up a cycle for the first time, this event is open to all — women and men. Our rides celebrate the spirit of empowerment, community, and environmental consciousness.

The event is timed to coincide with three powerful global observances — World Milk Day, World Bicycle Day, and World Environment Day — making every kilometre ridden a statement for health, sustainability, and equality.
